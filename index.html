Who Am I?

Graphic & Media Designer

My enthusiasm for artistic endevours and passion for creativity led me to pursue a 4 year honours degree in Creative Digital Media at the Insitiute of Technology Blanchardstown. I graduated in September 2012, achieving first class honours and was recognised as having achieved the overall best final year project. Since then I have worked at Hot Press magazine as an in-house graphic & web designer. Currently I work full-time at USIT Travel, where I design both print and digital marketing materials. I enjoy everything to do with design and multimedia. My enthusiasm for the digital arts is boundless.
